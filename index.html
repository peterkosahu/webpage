WELCOME TO KOSA CONSULT

I specialize in guiding companies through the intricacies of digital transformation and uncovering the vast array of possibilities it offers. This involves not only formulating a comprehensive corporate digital strategy but also delving into the specifics of process optimization and the development of innovative products and services. With a wealth of experience spanning over 25 years in the IT sector, encompassing pivotal roles in software and hardware product development, company establishment, and extensive process optimization endeavors, I am well-equipped to seamlessly steer digitalization projects from inception to fruition, catering to startups as well as large enterprises.

Recognizing the unique nature of each business, I adopt a personalized approach to every project I undertake. I meticulously analyze and refine existing business processes, and where necessary, I create tailored digital solutions, be it through software customization or even bespoke development. Leveraging my expertise in hardware development, IoT, and ID automation technology, I integrate physical processes seamlessly into the digital realm, ensuring a harmonious fusion between the tangible and the digital worlds.
